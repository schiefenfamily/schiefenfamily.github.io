General Notes: Husband - Frederick "Friedrich" Wilhelm "William" Schiefen



Immigrated to Perkinsville, Steuben County , NY on February 29,1872, at the age of 31.  He came to the US with  a minor For whom he was the guardian.  Peter B Schiefen son of Joseph Herman Schiefen.  He ( Friedrich )  was a private escort of KaiserWilliam. He lived in Perkinsville, New York where he had a blacksmith shop.  On April 5, 1884 Friederich and Catherine and the four children moved to a farm in Lynn Township, Sioux County, Iowa  near Hospers.  They lived on this farm 2 years during which time Charles and Helen were born.  They then move to the town of Hospers, Iowa where they lived for one year. They then moved to Alton,  Iowa where they lived for 2 years. He worked in a mill there and George was born in 1887.  In July 1889 they moved by covered wagon to a farm 7 miles northeast of Hawarden, Iowa in Eagle Township in Sioux County.  There Chris was born in 1889, Frank in 1891, Julius in 1894, and Catherine in1898. In Feb 1906 they moved to Goodland , Kansas where they bought a farm and lived until Sept 1911 when they moved back to the farm in Eagle Township, Sioux County , Iowa.  In March 1919 they bought a home in Hawarden and lived there until Catherine died on July 14, 1922.  Friederich then moved to the country and made his home with his daugher-in-law, Margaret Schiefen. He died May 7,1928 in Eagle Township, Sioux County, Iowa.
William, Anna, Joe, and Theodore were born in Stueben County jn NY and George, Chris, Frank,Julius, and Catherine were born in Sioux Couinty Iowa.
